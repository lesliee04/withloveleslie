  About Us

Hello, my name is Leslie, and I am currently based in New York. From a young age, I have had a deep passion for photography and videography, which have become my primary means of self-expression. My sister, Ashley, has always enjoyed creating and adding her unique touch to her crafts. Professionally, I work at a rental property management company, where I have gained nearly three years of experience and developed the skills necessary to take on a managerial role. In late 2024, my younger sister, Ashley, and I decided to start a side business selling baked goods. We also have future plans to expand our offerings to include jewelry handcrafted by Ashley and potentially launch a photobooth business. Although these goals may sometimes seem far-fetched, Ashley and I are determined to make them a reality. Coming from a Hispanic background, our dreams are often dismissed by others. Despite this, we hope our page and story will encourage other Hispanic kids like us to start their own businesses and share their talents with the world.
