  About Us

Hello, my name is Leslie, and I currently reside in New York. From a young age, I have always had a profound interest in photography and videography. These passions have evolved into my primary forms of self-expression.

In late 2024, my younger sister, Ashley, and I decided to start a side business selling baked goods. We also have future plans to expand our offerings to include jewelry handcrafted by Ashley and potentially launch a photobooth business. Although these goals may sometimes seem far-fetched, Ashley and I are determined to make them a reality. Coming from a Hispanic background, our dreams are often dismissed by others. Despite this, we hope our page and story will encourage other Hispanic kids like us to start their own businesses and share their talents with the world.
